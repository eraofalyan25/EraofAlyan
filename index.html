DEUTSCH · GERMANY

Why German? Why Germany?

Germany runs the largest economy in Europe, trains more engineers than it can hire, and largely funds its public universities with tax money - not tuition. German is the key that opens all three doors at once.

  • 01

    Tuition-free or low-cost public universities

    Most public German universities charge no tuition - German fluency is usually what stands between you and that door.

  • 02

    A labour shortage Germany openly talks about

    Engineering, healthcare, and IT roles regularly go unfilled, and visa pathways exist because the gap is real - easier with German B1+.

  • 03

    The largest economy in the EU

    More headquarters, more suppliers, more openings - for people who can operate in German.

  • 04

    A second language that compounds with English

    English plus German is a different CV than English alone.

ONE TEST · FOUR SKILLS

Why IELTS?

IELTS is the one English score that universities, employers and immigration authorities across dozens of countries agree to read the same way.

Students applying abroad

UK, Canada, Australia, and English-taught programs in Germany and Europe almost all ask for an IELTS score.

Skilled migration applicants

Canada's Express Entry and Australia's skilled visas score English proficiency directly from your IELTS band.

Job seekers & professionals

Multinational employers and registration bodies often ask for a minimum band as part of licensing.
